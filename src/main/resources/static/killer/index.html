Killer

Rules

(expand)

In killer each player is given a certain number of lives (normally 3).

Enter the name of each player in the "Add Player" section. Once you have entered the name of each player press start.

Every turn a player is randomly picked from the "To Be Drawn" list, this player must then take a shot.

If the player is unavailable you can press "Replace" to select another player.

The player must now attempt to pot a ball.

  • If they pot press the "Pot" button, this puts their life through to the next round.
  • If they miss press the "Miss" button, this removes their life.
  • If they gain lives enter the number they gained and press "Add Lives".
    A player gains a life for potting the black and an extra life for every ball they pot after the first, however a fowl subtracts one life.

    • If you make a mistake you can correct it by using the "Promote", "Demote" and "X" buttons.

  • "Promote" moves that life from the "To Be Drawn" section to the "Through" section.
  • "Demote" moves that life from the "Through" section to the "To Be Drawn" section
  • "X" removes that life from the game,
    • You can also add lives back in using the "Add Player" section as before.

    The winner is the last player standing
